Introduction to Magic in Erilea

Magic in the world of Erilea is an ancient and powerful force that flows through the landand certain individuals. This documentation provides comprehensive technicalinformation on the manifestation, manipulation, and application of magical energieswithin the Throne of Glass universe. Magic users must understand that wielding such power comes with responsibility andconsequences. This documentation serves as the official reference guide forpractitioners, scholars, and students of magic across the realms.

Key Principles

The fundamental principle of magic in Erilea is that it exists as a natural energy force that certain individuals can tap into and manipulate. The strength of one's magical abilities depends on:

  • Bloodline and Inheritance
  • Training and Practice
  • Mental Fortitude
  • Physical Strength
  • Environmental Factors
  • Connection to Magical Sources (e.g., Wyrdkeys, Wyrdgates)
Types of Magic

Erilea features several distinct magical classifications, each with unique properties andapplications:

  • Raw Magic- The purest form of magical energy, often manifested as fire
  • Fae Magic- Ancestral magic of the Fae race with elemental affinities
  • Valg Magic- Dark, corrupting magic originating from another realm
  • Witch Magic- Iron-based magic wielded by the Ironteeth and Crochan witches
  • Wyrd Magic- Ancient magic tied to the fabric of worlds
  • Healing Magic- Restorative energies focused on physical and spiritual healing
  • Shapeshifting- Physical transformation abilities
  • Glamours- Illusion-based magic affecting perception
Raw Magic Manipulation

Raw magic is the foundation of most magical practices in Erilea. Practitioners can manifest this energy through proper technique and control.

Incantation Format

       
       <Element> + <Intent> + <Magnitude> = <Effect>

Control Mechanisms

Raw magic requires disciplined mental control to prevent burnout. The practitioner must maintain balance between:

  1. Magical Ouput (power expended)
  2. Physical Endurance (body's limitation)
  3. Mental Focus (concentration)
  4. Environmental Magic Availability (location-based)
  5. Emotional State (affects magic stability)

Valg Magic Corruption Protocols

Valg magic represents a corrupting force that operates by specific technical rules.Understanding these systems is crucial for defense and countermeasures.

Corruption Stages

  • Initial Contact- First exposure to Valg magic
  • Surface Infiltration- Magic begins affecting outward behavior
  • Essence Compromise- Internal corruption begins
  • Will Subjugation- Host's will becomes subdued
  • Complete Possession- Full control by Valg entity

Corruption Defense Measures

  1. Maintain strong mental barriers through meditation
  2. Utilize protective amulets containing specific minerals
  3. Regular cleansing rituals using salt, iron, and flowing water
  4. Avoidance of direct eye contact with corrupted entities
  5. Application of sacred symbols at key body points

Valg Magaical Signature Detection

Magic Ethics and Safety Protocols

The ethical use of magic and proper safety procedures are essential for all practitioners.

Core Ethical Guidelines

  1. Respect all sentient life
  2. Obtain consent before magical intervention
  3. Maintain balance in natural systems
  4. Take responsibility for magical consequences
  5. Protect knowledge from misuse
  6. Prioritize non-magical solutions when appropriate
  7. Report dangerous magical anomalies

Safety Protocols

  • Always establish containment before experimental magic
  • Maintain energy reserves for emergency cancellation
  • Use proper protective equipment for hazardous magic
  • Work with a spotter for dangerous procedures
  • Document all new magical discoveries
  • Perform regular magical health assessments
  • Establish fail-safes for sustained magical operations
